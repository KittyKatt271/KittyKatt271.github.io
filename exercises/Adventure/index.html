The New Path

As a traveling merchant, you are no stranger to the road. However, upon your usual
route to the Castle Town Market, you notice something odd.

A new road, so new it has yet to be cobbled, has branched off from the main road.

You can't help but to wonder, “How curious?”

Do you keep to the main road?       Or does your curiosity get the best of you?

The Main Road

While venturing upon the new path could lead to somewhere new - an adventure just waiting to begin - this is not prudent. You know the best way to sell your wares is to head to the Castle Town Market. Letting out a sigh of disappointment, you venture forth upon the main road.

Just as always, you soon find yourself upon the outer gates of the Castle Town Market. The guards, recognizing you, immediately open up the gates. You proceed through them and on to your usual spot.

As you set up your booth, you can't help but wonder what lies beyond the road that could have been.

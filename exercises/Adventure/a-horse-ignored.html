A Horse Ignored

“Sssshhh….” You whisper, in an attempt to comfort your horse.

But your pleas to calm him, they are not working. Whatever lies beyond that path, has spooked the poor thing beyond imagining. With a fright this tremendous - and one that came with a disturbing wind, no less - couldn't be good.

Clearly, this is a warning. But…

There is just something…

Something about this road that just can't be ignored.

You tie off your horse to a nearby tree and head once more towards the new path.

As you step off the main road, you turn your head to gaze back once more. Your steed, still frightened, fights his restraints and whinnies.

Are you sure you want to venture forth?               It's not too late to turn back…

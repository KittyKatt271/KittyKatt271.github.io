It's Too Late

“Hmm… Maybe You should…”

Before you can complete your thought, a feeling of dread washes over you and a wind as black as the unseen void blows past, eradicating the main road and your caravan.

You quickly look around. The world around you seems to have disappeared, even the ground itself. All that remains is the road you're standing - this new path.

With nowhere else to go, you venture forth hoping it leads back to the realm you knew. But as you progress, the surrounding nothingness only seems to darken and the sense of dread only grows.

Welcome to the void.

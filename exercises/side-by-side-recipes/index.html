Japanese Creme Filled Sandwiches

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 40 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Yields: 10

These deep-fried sandwiches are inspired by a viral Japanese shop that had me absolutely hooked the moment I laid eyes on them. Golden, crispy but fluffy milk bread stuffed with whipped cream and strawberries or topped with custard and caramelized sugar for a crème brûlée twist— what is there not to love? This is my version of that dreamy dessert without the whole flight across the world to Japan!

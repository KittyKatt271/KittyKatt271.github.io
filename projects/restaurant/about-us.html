About Us

Tree House Tales began because living in the heart of the big city, we realized we were missing the serene beauty of the nature we once came from.

At Tree House Tales every table is designed to resemble a specific element of nature while the lighting is set to a soft glow. This is all done to amplify the magic that resides in the natural world, creating a soothing aura for the adults and a sense of whimsy for the children.

Our hope is that every single one of our customers leaves regaining a piece of the magic that had been lost in the bricks of downtown.
